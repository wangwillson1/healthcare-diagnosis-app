MedAI

The future of healthcare diagnosis

About MedAI

MedAI is a multipurpose healthcare diagnosis application that uses a machine learning algorithm to accurately predict physical and mental health disorders.

How it Works

MedAI predicts physical and mental health disorders by analyzing pictures of skin conditions or facial expressions respectively. Our machine learning algorithm determines the severity of the case and whether medical treatment is necessary.

Current Prototype

Our current prototype uses tens of thousands of photos of various conditions to train the machine learning model. The algorithm then uses this model to analyze a photo taken from your smartphone to predict the condition.

Currently, MedAI is able to predict whether the patient is at risk of depression or skin cancer.
